# Points & Ranking Lists

In the 2024-25 season, Mass Nordic adopted a points-based system to select teams for the Eastern High School (EHS) and U16 Championships.

Following the 2026 MA Nordic Cup, the MA Ranking List (MRL) will be posted here and updated regularly.

For information on how points and ranking lists are calculated, see [About MA Nordic Points]({% link points/about.md %}).
