# MA Nordic Cup

The MA Nordic Cup is the primary means of qualifying for a championship team.
It is held on a single day annually, typically in late January, and generally includes both a skate and a classic race.
Except in extenuating circumstances such as illness or injury, athletes attempting to qualify for a championship team are expected to race in the MA Nordic Cup.

## 2026 MA Nordic Cup

The 2026 MA Nordic Cup is the evening of Fri, Jan. 23, 2026, at Weston Ski Track in Weston, MA.

### Event Details

**Date:** Friday, January 23, 2026  
**Venue:** Leo J. Martin Golf Course and Ski Track, Weston, MA  

### Schedule

[View start list]({% link assets/pdf/2026/2026_MassNordicStartList_ByBib.pdf%}){: .btn .btn-blue}

- 5:30-7:00 pm: Bib pickup & U16/EHS forms and fees drop-off  
- 5:30-6:30 pm: Girls’ ski drop-off  
- 5:45-6:45 pm: Boys’ ski drop-off  
- 7:30 pm: **Classic 5.5k interval start** begins. Girls go first immediately followed by boys.

### Race Format

- The race will be **classic only**. There will be no skate race.
- Race distance is 4 laps, about 5.5km.
- 15 second interval start.

### Wax Protocol

Every racer will be on the same wax protocol. Racers should drop off their skis to the central wax support team upon arrival to the venue.

Prepare your skis with **base-level paraffin waxes only**. Approved wax lines include Star Next, Toko BP, Swix PS or CH, or Rode R-line. A standard liquid top coat will be applied to all skis at ski drop-off.

Suggested waxes:
- Star NF Cold or Polar  
- Toko BP Blue  
- Swix PS/CH 5 or 4  
- Rode R20  

### Refunds and Waivers

- **Waivers:** Athletes with an immovable prior commitment for Friday evening may file a waiver. Coaches should submit the waiver on the athlete’s behalf via [this form](https://forms.gle/hAyZnqmydJDZhb5L7).  
- **Refunds** are available and must be requested prior to race start. Refunded athletes will not be considered for U16/EHS teams.  
- **BKL athletes** not in contention for U16 Championships may receive a refund if unable to attend.
