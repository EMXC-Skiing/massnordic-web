# 2026 MA Nordic Cup

The 2026 MA Nordic Cup was held Fri, Jan. 23, 2026, at Weston Ski Track in Weston, MA. Due to weather and time constraints, the rescheduled event consisted of a single 5.5k classic interval start race.

## Race schedule

- 5:30-7:00 pm: Bib pickup & U16/EHS forms and fees drop-off  
- 5:30-6:30 pm: Girls’ ski drop-off  
- 5:45-6:45 pm: Boys’ ski drop-off  
- 7:30 pm: **Classic 5.5k interval start** begins. Girls go first immediately followed by boys.
